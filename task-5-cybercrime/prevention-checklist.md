🛡️Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails
Always verify the sender before sharing any personal information
Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

💰 Financial / UPI Safety:
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
Always verify payment requests before approving them
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or transactions

🌐 General Safety:
Check website URLs carefully before entering sensitive information
Log out from accounts when using shared devices
Be cautious while downloading files or apps from unknown sources

🚨 Reporting Cybercrime:
Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
