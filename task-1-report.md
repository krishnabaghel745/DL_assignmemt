Tool Used
I used Canva to create my Digital Literacy infographic.
It is a simple and user-friendly design tool with ready-made templates.
What My Design Shows
My infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students.
It includes key topics such as:
Meaning of digital literacy
Useful digital tools like Google Docs and Canva
Safe internet practices such as strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links
Professional online presence using platforms like LinkedIn
Basic email etiquette for formal communication
I used icons, colors, and short bullet points to make the information easy to understand and visually attractive.
🔹 What I Learned
I learned how to present information in a visual and concise format.
I understood the importance of design and clarity in communication.
🔹 Challenge / Interesting Part
Initially, it was difficult to choose the right layout and balance between text and visuals.
It was interesting to use icons and design elements to make the infographic more engaging
