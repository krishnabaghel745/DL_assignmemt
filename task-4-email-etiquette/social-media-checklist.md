✅ Do’s
Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments
Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings
Share useful and positive content
Think before posting anything online
Maintain a professional image on public profiles

❌ Don’ts
Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
Do not engage in online arguments or bullying
Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information
Do not use informal or abusive language in professional spaces
